bioParagraph1: "Dewi Ria Utari, popularly known as Dru, is an Indonesian author of mystical realism fiction. Born in Malang, she currently resides in Jakarta with her husband and two children."
bioParagraph2: "Dru began writing professionally in 2003, when her short stories were regularly published in the literary pages of Kompas daily newspaper. Her debut novel, Rumah Hujan (House of Rain), published in 2012, immediately became a top 10 bestseller, admired for its unique blend of magical realism and Javanese mythology."
bioParagraph3: "Throughout her career, Dru has explored themes of identity, memory, and the supernatural through prose that bridges traditional storytelling with contemporary literary sensibilities. Her works have been recognized with nominations for major Indonesian literary awards, cementing her position as one of the country's distinguished voices in fiction."
